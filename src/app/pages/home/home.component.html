Product Description

Truswitch is an intelligent IoT device that replaces your traditional switch, avoiding the need for costly rewiring. It authenticates users and monitors power consumption, delivering up to 3.3KW output. It’s compact (20mm x 50mm x 40mm), and fits perfectly into your switch box. Any qualified electrician can install it, and you can set it up in minutes with our easy-to-follow guide.

  • Ensures high safety levels
  • Certified by BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards)
  • Automatically shuts off power during high voltage/temperature/current situations
  • Automatically cuts power during prolonged usage
  • Compatible with all standard sockets
  • Functions with or without internet connectivity
  • Mobile app offers various configuration options tailored to your society's requirements
